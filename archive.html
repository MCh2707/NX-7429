Welcome to the CYRANET Document Management System

This portal provides authorized personnel and FOIA-eligible parties access to institutional records produced by the Cyranet Research Institute between 1973 and 1979. Records include administrative documents, facility maintenance logs, personnel transfers, budget allocations, and selected research summaries.

Use the folder browser on the left to navigate available collections. Click any document title to open a scanned record. Documents marked RESTRICTED require Level 4 clearance or a pending FOIA request approval.

NOTICE: Several records from the period 1977–1979 are currently under administrative review. Access to these files may be limited. For inquiries, contact the Office of Records and Compliance at ext. 4401.
